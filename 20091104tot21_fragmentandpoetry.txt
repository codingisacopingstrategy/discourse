Even though there is no meaning intrinsic to life, the production of meaning is intrinsic to living. Eric is in relentless pursuit of meaning and its creation. He often ends up where the mind goes in circles. But what does that mean, a mind going in circles? It only means that one keeps revisiting the same words over and over. But there are not so many words; there are much more things. And since time and causality keep the things in a constant state of flux, the same words are always different, as they refer to different things. Once you add in the dimension of time, the motion is not circular, it is spiralling. Like an equation without a solution; like a function that never finishes. Words give the comforting sensation of stability, they give a semblance of permanence but like the Greek guy already said you never step into the same river twice.
